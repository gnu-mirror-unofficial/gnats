  • To: gnats-devel at sources dot redhat dot com,bug-gnats at gnu dot org

Time permitting, I will try to kick out a more or less "final" 2.x version 
of Gnatsweb. This will be a bug-fix release only, and there will have to be 
some mighty good reasons if I am to implement anything entirely new or make 
big structural changes. The Gnatsweb contained in the Gnats 4.0 
distribution will no longer work with 3.x servers.

In order to do this, I need help. I will go through the mailing list 
archives and pick up whatever patches I can find there, but I am quite 
certain that people have unsubmitted 2.x patches lying around. Please send 
them to the mailing list within a week or two (preferably patches against 
2.7 beta), and I will try to get a Gnatsweb 2.8.0 out of the door.
