I don't know if you found it yourself, but there is actually a fairly 
well-working web-interface for Gnats administration, developed by Mike 
Sutton, described in the mailing list archives. Have a look at 
http://sources.redhat.com/ml/bug-gnats/1999-07/msg00067.html

I got it working very easily, and it performed quite well during some, 
admittedly limited, testing. I does seem to have certain problems with 
blank lines in config files (leading, for example, to the addition of 
no-name categories), but these should be fairly straightforward to fix. 
Multiple database support should be fairly easy to add by some advanced 
cutting and pasting from Gnatsweb. It could also be modified to work with 
Gnats 4 fairly easily

What needs to be addressed is security.

Would any of the Savannah people be interested in picking up this and 
developing it further (I am assuming here that Mr. Sutton has no objections) ?
