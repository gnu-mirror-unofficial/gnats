Major Gnatsweb commit

  • To: gnats-devel at sources dot redhat dot com
  • Subject: Major Gnatsweb commit
  • From: Yngve Svendsen <yngve dot svendsen at clustra dot com>
  • Date: Sun, 08 Jul 2001 01:41:46 +0200
Today, I committed Dirk Bergstrom's jumbo Gnatsweb patch for the v4 branch 
of Gnatsweb to the new Gnatsweb repository (see 
http://sources.redhat.com/ml/gnats-devel/2001-q3/msg00000.html).

The ChangeLog can be found at 
http://sources.redhat.com/ml/gnats-devel/2001-q2/msg00027.html

The patch makes Gnatsweb much cleaner as a Perl program, it makes it a 
great deal more robust and far more customizable. All in all, it brings us 
near the goal of a production-quality Gnatsweb for Gnats 4. However, three 
or four medium-impact bugs remain, and the primary focus will be on getting 
these fixed soon.

Yngve Svendsen
Gnatsweb maintainer
