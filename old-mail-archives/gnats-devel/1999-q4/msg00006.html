I actually think tkgnats is mature enough as-is to stand on its own.
I think it's reasonable to place documentation in the contrib area
advertising and suggesting the use of tkgnats,  but it tends to
evolve and improve at a different pace than gnats,  so it makes
sense to break 'em apart.
