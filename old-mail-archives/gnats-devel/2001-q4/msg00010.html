At 08:02 02.10.2001 -0400, Perry Klein wrote:
>Gnats is currently being evaluated for use at our Lab.  Does Gnats have 
>the capability to modify( add and/or change) the enumeration values of a 
>field or add new fields to the PR's?

In the current version 3.113.1 of GNATS, some enumerated field values are 
configurable, such as the values for the "state" field, but the 
posibilities are very limited. Adding fields is a difficult exercise.

However, in the fairly-soon-to-be-released version 4 of GNATS, absolutely 
everything is fully configurable. Have a look at the preliminary version of 
the GNATS 4 manual at http://sources.redhat.com/gnats/4_0_manual/, 
especially section 4.3, The dbconfig file, and you should get an idea of 
how configurable this thing really is.

GNATS 4 is currently only available from CVS, details can be found under 
"Availability" at http://sources.redhat.com/gnats/, a late alpha or beta 
tarball may be available fairly soon.
