I finally got my Gnats 4 install notes typed up in a form that is readable. 
The how-to is loosely based on the Gnats 3 install documentation, but all 
those subtle, hard-to-ferret-out differences are now (hopefully) 
documented. The document is still in a very early stage, but it should take 
those people who want to try Gnats 4 through the hardest parts of the 
installation.

The URL is http://sources.redhat.com/gnats/gnats4inst.html

And in case people are wondering: Gnats 4 is actually quite usable in its 
current state.
